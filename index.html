London Analysis and Probability Seminar

The London Analysis and Probability seminar is jointly organized and is alternately held by the Analysis groups of Imperial College London (IC), King's College London (KCL), Queen Mary University of London (QMUL) and University College London (UCL).

The seminar meets during term time on Thursdays. The meetings are open to everyone. In some cases registration may be required due to access restrictions to buildings.

To subscribe (or unsubscribe) to the London Analysis mailing list, please use www.jiscmail.ac.uk/london-analysis-mailing-list
