The Paris-London Analysis Seminar

The seminar meets four times a year, usually June in Paris, September in London, December in Paris, March in London, for a one-day session of four talks.

The next session will be held in London on Friday November 7, 2025
at King's College, London


This seminar will be part of two-days, November 6 and 7, dedicated to the memory Brian Davies and jointly organized with the London Analysis and Probability Seminar.
