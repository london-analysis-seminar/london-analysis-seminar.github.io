Ruoyu Wang Yale, University College London

Fractional Laplacians: propagation, delocalisation, and evolution

Abstract:

In this talk, we will survey some results around this question, and connect such eigenstate concentration to scars and chaos in quantum systems. We then discuss some new propagation and tunnelling results for fractional Laplacians, with their application in obtaining decay estimates for linearised gravity and capillary water waves. Those are joint works with Perry Kleinhenz (Illinois State), and with Jian Wang (IHÉS).
