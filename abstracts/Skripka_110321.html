Anna Skripka (University of New Mexico)

Approximation of functions of differential operators

Abstract:
We will discuss recent advancements in approximation of operator functions that arise in problems of mathematical physics and noncommutative geometry. Those questions reduce to finding nice bounds and representations for (semi)-norms of approximation errors. Challenges arise, in particular, from noncommutativity of a perturbation and initial unbounded operator and from noncompactness of the perturbation. Successful resolution rests on multilinear operator integration, a powerful technical method with a long history in noncommutative analysis.
