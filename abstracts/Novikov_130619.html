Roman Novikov (CNRS, École Polytechnique)

Moutard type transformations for generalized analytic functions and for the conductivity equation

Abstract:
The transformations of the Darboux-Moutard type go back to publications [ Moutard, 1878], [Darboux 1882]. Recently, we have constructed and studied Moutard type transformations for generalized analytic functions (that is for the Carleman system or Bers-Vekua system) and further for the conductivity equation. This talk is based, in particular, on works [Grinevich, Novikov, 2016, 2019], [ Novikov, Taimanov, 2016, 2018].
