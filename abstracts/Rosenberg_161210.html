Steve Rosenberg (Boston University):

Title: Pseudodifferential operators in infinite dimensional geometry

Abstract:
Many infinite dimensional manifolds such as Diff(M), the diffeomorphism group of a manifold, or LM, the loop space of a manifold, have gauge groups G as the structure group of their tangent bundles. To discuss geometric properties of these manifolds, it is useful to enlarge the structure group to a group of pseudodifferential operators. This allows us to define characteristic classes of certain infinite dimensional bundles and to detect elements in the cohomology of the classifying spaces BG and BP.
