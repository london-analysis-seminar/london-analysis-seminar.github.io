Alberto Ruiz (Universidad Autonoma de Madrid)

Hardy uncertainty principle for covariant Schrödinger flows

Abstract: The key point is a logarithmic convexity result for exponentially weighted L2-norms of solutions to electromagnetic Schrödinger equation. We obtain as a consequence unique continuation of the type of Hardy uncertainty principle. Joint work with J. Barceló, S. Gutierrez, L. Fanelli and M. Vilela.
