Title: Semiconcavity of the square distance in Carnot groups.

Abstract: Semiconcavity and semiconvexity are key regularity properties for functions with many applications in a broad range of mathematical subjects. The notions of semiconcavity and semiconvexity have been adapted to different geometrical contexts, in particular in sub-Riemannian structures such as Carnot groups, where they turn out to be extremely useful for the study of solutions of degenerate PDEs.
