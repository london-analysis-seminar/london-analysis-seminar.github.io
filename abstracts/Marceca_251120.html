Spectral deviation of localisation operators and applications
Abstract:

This talk focuses on the eigenvalue distribution of localisation operators of the form $P\chi_\Omega P$, where $P$ is the orthogonal projection onto a reproducing kernel Hilbert spaceÂ and $\Omega$ is a compact domain. We estimate how much the corresponding eigenvalue counting function deviates from the measure of the localisation domain. Our results are quantitative both in terms of the size of the domain and the spectral threshold of the eigenvalue counting function. We discuss applications to signal processing and mathematical physics enabled by this two-parameter control.
